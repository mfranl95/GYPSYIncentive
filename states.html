Gypsy Incentive - The Branch of Georgia

The Peach State

Gypsy Incentive-Georgia targets high school students in the Atlanta area that would like to gain their high school diploma. For several years, the drop-out rate for high schoolers in Georgia were at an all-time high. With the help of GYPSY Incentive, we were able to raise the graduation rate to 77 percent just last year.

Gypsy Incentive - The Branch of Missouri

The Show-Me State

Gypsy Incentive-Missouri targets high school students in the Illinois area that would like to gain their high school diploma. GYPSY Incentive has helped raise the graduation rate to an average of 87.8 percent in 2016, raising the graduation rate up 87.3 percent from the previous school year. Missouri is now one of the top-ten states in the US with a high high school graduation rate.
